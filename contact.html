FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join HER Circle?
HER Circle welcomes women who own or are building a business, have professional goals, and believe in supporting other women's success — entrepreneurs, professionals, executives, and emerging leaders alike.
How much does membership cost?
We offer tiered membership options, and our Founders Scholarship covers membership for women facing financial barriers. Reach out through the form above and we'll share current pricing.
Are events open to non-members?
Many are! Most networking events welcome guests, while some workshops and roundtables are member-exclusive. Each event page notes who can attend.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
HER Circle is a registered nonprofit organization. You'll receive a receipt for every gift; consult your tax advisor regarding deductibility in your situation.
How do I become a mentor or speaker?
We'd love that. Select "Volunteering" in the contact form or sign up on our Support Us page, and our programs team will follow up with next steps.
