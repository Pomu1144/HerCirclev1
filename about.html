About HER Circle

Built on the Belief That Women Rise Together

A community of ambitious women, entrepreneurs, professionals, and business owners who believe in the power of connection, collaboration, and mutual support.

Our Story

A circle, not a ladder.

HER Circle began with a simple observation: women everywhere are building businesses, leading organizations, launching ideas, and creating impact - often without the network that makes the journey easier. We registered as a nonprofit to change that.

We bring together women who are driven, passionate, and committed to their goals - and to each other's. Through meaningful relationships, education, mentorship, business development, and community engagement, HER Circle helps every member expand her network, increase her visibility, and access the opportunities that fuel success.

Our Mission

Empower ambitious women.

To empower ambitious women by creating a supportive community that fosters business growth, leadership development, collaboration, and lasting connections.

Our Vision

A trusted, influential network.

To become a trusted and influential network where women can grow their businesses, strengthen their leadership, build meaningful relationships, and achieve their ambitions together.

Who We Serve

HER Circle is for women who...

  • Own or are building a business

  • Want to connect with like-minded women
